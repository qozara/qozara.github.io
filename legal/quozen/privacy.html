Privacy Policy: Quozen

Last Updated: March 2026

Quozen is an open-source project by Qozara. This policy supplements the Master Privacy Policy.

1. Data Storage

Quozen is decentralized. All data is stored in your personal Google Drive. Qozara does not host your data.

2. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Quozen's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. AI Data Handling

If using Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) features, your data is sent to your chosen AI provider (e.g., Google Gemini) according to their terms. Your keys are encrypted and stored in your own Google Drive.
