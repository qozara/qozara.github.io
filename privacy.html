Qozara Master Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Qozara is a research and experimentation collective. We believe in privacy by design and data sovereignty.

1. General Principles

We do not sell, rent, or monetize user data. Our projects prioritize decentralized storage where users retain full ownership of their information.

2. Specific Applications

Individual projects under the Qozara umbrella may have specific data handling requirements. Please refer to the /legal/ directory for app-specific policies.

3. Contact

Inquiries regarding privacy across all Qozara projects can be sent to legal@qozara.org.
